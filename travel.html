景点名称 布鲁威斯号

所在城市 / 国家：山东威海

I visited Weihai this time and saw the famous Bruwis, which was really shocking The Bruwis is a Panama-registered empty bulk carrier, 190 meters long and 31 meters wide, with a loading capacity of up to 60,000 tons. In September 2022, affected by the outer circulation of Typhoon "Nanmadol", it ran aground in the waters of Haoyunjiao Tourist Resort, Rongcheng City, Weihai. Originally destined for retirement, dismantling, or resale, it unexpectedly became popular due to the grounding. Since spring 2023, with the help of social media, it has attracted large numbers of tourists specifically to "punch in." During the 2024 Spring Festival holiday, it received an average of over 12,000 visitors daily, with peaks exceeding 30,000. The ship is perfect for photos. Its rusty hull exudes industrial beauty, suitable for any photography style. At sunrise, golden light spills over the ship; at sunset, the evening glow makes the scene breathtaking. Moreover, the romantic seaside atmosphere here is palpable—sea breeze whispers, waves lap against the ship and beach, and strolling around makes time seem to slow down. Surrounding facilities are constantly improving, with a sand sculpture art park, ship restaurant, and planned projects like internet-famous homestays and cafes. When visiting, you can watch the sunrise in the morning, enjoy seafood nearby at noon, explore surrounding attractions in the afternoon, and return to admire the sunset in the evening. The Bruwis is truly worth a visit. Its unique beauty and story are unforgettable.

旅行时间：2025年01月
