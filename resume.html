个人简历

基本信息

大家好！欢迎来到我的个人网站，我的名字是刘霁千，我来自江西九江，是一个性格开朗活泼的女生。这座被庐山云雾缭绕、鄱阳湖碧波环抱的城市，赋予了我对自然与生活的无限热爱。自小在长江边奔跑嬉戏，听着船笛与渔歌长大，让我骨子里刻满了对探索未知的渴望。

学生时代，我始终保持着对知识的热忱，擅长用逻辑思维拆解难题，也热爱在文字与艺术中寻找灵感。在小组合作中不断打磨专业技能，更享受与团队协作攻克难关的成就感。

生活里，我是个“斜杠青年”：热衷徒步旅行，用脚步丈量过川西高原的苍茫、云南古镇的静谧；闲暇时会沉浸在丙烯画的世界，用色彩记录转瞬即逝的灵感；也常在周末约上三五好友，在桌游与露营中释放活力。

我相信，每个真诚的相遇都是命运的礼物。希望通过这个网站，能与志同道合的你分享生活的点滴，也期待聆听属于你的故事！

最喜欢的一句话

我步入丛林，因为我希望生活得有意义，我希望活得深刻，并汲取生命中所有的精华。–––《瓦尔登湖》

  不是逃离生活，而是主动「扎进」生活的丛林，哪怕会遇到荆棘（比如徒步时的暴雨、丙烯画创作的瓶颈），也要把日子过出「深刻的质感」，榨取每一份体验的「精华」；

  这本书时，我正为「要不要跳出舒适圈」纠结，这句话突然点醒我：所谓「有意义的生活」，不是等出来的，是像梭罗那样，主动走进「丛林」（不管是真实的自然，还是人生的挑战），去折腾、去浸泡、去提炼属于自己的答案 。

兴趣爱好

My hobbies are as diverse as a vibrant garden, where each interest is a precious gift from life, making ordinary days shine with extraordinary colors. In the digital realm, I’m a fearless "adventurer." I’m passionate about role-playing games—galloping across the vast landscapes of Hyrule in The Legend of Zelda, solving ancient temple puzzles, or teaming up with friends in the峡谷 of League of Legends to experience the thrill of collaborative strategy. Gaming isn’t just entertainment for me; it’s a journey of self-discovery and boundless exploration. Stepping into the real world, I transform into an energetic "ball chaser" on the badminton court. Every week, I meet friends to practice, swinging rackets, leaping, and smashing shuttlecocks, relishing the joy of sweating it out. The graceful arc of the shuttlecock in the air mirrors my fiery and dynamic love for sports. Reading serves as a sanctuary for my soul. In quiet moments, I immerse myself in books—traversing the pursuit of equality with Jane Eyre, embarking on magical adventures at Hogwarts with Harry Potter, or savoring life’s bittersweetness through Yu Hua’s characters. Each book is a doorway to a new world, helping me grow and find meaning between the lines. Music is the backdrop to my life. From the elegance of classical pieces to the passion of rock, the simplicity of folk to the trendiness of pop, every melody echoes my emotions. I start mornings with upbeat tunes and unwind at night with soothing rhythms, letting music color every mood. These diverse hobbies weave together the tapestry of my life, allowing me to find peace in busyness, grow through challenges, and embrace the world with an open heart.
